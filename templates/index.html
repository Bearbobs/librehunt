Why should I use Linux?

There's a distro for everyone. From the nerd to the newbie.

There's a type for everyone. Advanced Linux distro's for the advanced users, while there are beginner distros for beginners. Distros also differ in their values, such as Ubuntu making Proprietary software easy to use for the average user, and Arch Linux, an advanced distro Keeping It Simple (KISS)! They also differ in their desktops, so a distro can have the simple GNOME desktop, or a customizable KDE Plasma desktop, or something lightweight and completely scriptable desktop like i3! Something for everyone.

100% Secure. Check for yourself.

Unlike in Mac or Windows, you can have your cake and eat it too! While remaining free (freedom wise as well as price) You can rest be assured your data does not get leaked to big corporations. The same, however, cannot be said for Windows. Plus, if you doubt me, don't take my word for it. You can see the source code, used to make the different components of the various distro's as well as the distro's themselves online, with a community always on the look out for any backdoors or any security vulnerabilities of any kind. With Linux, you're secured for sure!

Use it anywhere. Includes pen drives.

Since Linux is free (free as in free speech as well as free as in free beer) you can make copies and share it with your friends and family, make your own too, and run it, share it, copy it everywhere! From supercomputers to simple pendrives, Linux is everywhere.
